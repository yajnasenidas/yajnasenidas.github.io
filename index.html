My Projects

As mentioned earlier, I have already briefed about my curriculum project, in precise right above. Talking about in detail, the topic of our project is Aerial Vehicle Search Path Optimisation.
Let us consider a disastrous situation, like that of a plane crash at sea where the only information about the crash site comes from the flight plan, maybe supplemented with a Mayday call. Among the survivors, some may have managed to get in a life raft, which may be drifting as it is subject to winds and currents. In such a case, it is not sufficient to just fully cover the search area, since it is possible for the life raft to move into an area that has already been observed. To increase the chances of survival, efficient planning methods are required, which can be done with the help of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs).
UAVs are nothing but a vehicle without any pilot onboard. Our Project, thus proposes an optimized search path for this UAV.
It accounts for a three-step model:
a)The Target Model: It is nothing but the target/ the destination to be reached.
b) The Sensor Model: It carries the sensors to take observations.
c)The Platform Model: The object to be tracked.
